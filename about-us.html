Our Mission

TThe Entrepreneurship Student Club @ CCNY is a student-run organization with the mission of fostering a space of interdisciplinary collab and connecting the campus and community.

Our community is full of engineers, scientists, economists, and psychologists who are aspiring entrepreneurs, innovators and leaders. ESC brings it's vision to life through a series of events, workshops, programs, and initiatives.
